About Us

Behold.

This is the most complete database of radio communications data in the realm, assembled not by chance, but by process.1

For many seasons, our Keepers of the Database, Ministers of Submission Review, and the Supreme Curator of Trunked Things have processed well over a million user offerings. Each submission is received with gratitude, reviewed with seriousness, and then passed through a series of ancient and highly respected administrative rituals, the details of which are no longer discussed.2

Through this sacred bureaucracy, we have curated the largest, most complete, and most unquestionably authoritative source of radio communications and trunked radio systems known to exist.

Authority, after all, is not something one earns.
It is something one declares.

“Our strength lies in consistency. We do the same thing every time, regardless of circumstance.”
— Senior Vice Steward of Policy

Every piece of data is lovingly cataloged, occasionally corrected, and, when necessary, deemed not quite right, in accordance with guidelines established long ago by people who are no longer reachable.3

“We value community input greatly. That’s why we’ve built an extensive process to protect it from being used incorrectly.”
— Director of Community Alignment

Many have questioned how such a vast repository could be maintained with such confidence. The answer is simple: we have procedures. And when procedures are followed precisely, the outcome is always correct — even when it isn’t.

“Change introduces uncertainty. We prefer certainty.”
— Acting Deputy Assistant to the Chief of Stability

Ethical Sourcing

We are proud to confirm that all frequencies within our database are humanely and ethically sourced.

No frequency is harvested prematurely.
No signal is taken without cause.
And no waveform is processed unless it has lived a full and meaningful broadcast life.4

Our commitment to ethical sourcing is embodied in our guiding principle:

Tower-to-Table™

From the moment a frequency leaves the tower, it is treated with dignity and respect. Our specialists ensure it is:

  • Carefully received
  • Gently interpreted
  • Thoroughly inspected
  • And finally placed into a properly aligned database table where it may rest among its peers

At no point is a frequency rushed, mishandled, or — heaven forbid — joined incorrectly.5

“You can taste the difference when a frequency has been responsibly tabulated.”
— Head of Signal Provenance

Our Philosophy

Today, our database stands as a monument to order, a triumph of administration, and a shining example of what can be achieved when enthusiasm is carefully managed.

Should disagreements arise, rest assured they will be addressed promptly, respectfully, and after proper review.6
